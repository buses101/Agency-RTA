The Riverside Transit Agency (RTA), established in 1975, is the primary transit provider for western Riverside County, California.
RTA operates a comprehensive network of services, including 32 local routes, three CommuterLink express routes, on-demand GoMicro microtransit service, and Dial-A-Ride services.

The agency serves a population of over one million people and is funded by various sources, including the Federal Transit Administration and the California Department of Transportation.
RTA is governed by a board of directors comprising 22 elected officials from 18 cities in western Riverside County and four members of the County Board of Supervisors.
The agency is committed to providing safe, reliable, and accessible transportation options for all residents in its service area.
